Account & Registration

How do I create an account?
Click on "Register" in the top menu, fill in your details, and verify your email address. You can start shopping immediately after registration.

Do I need an account to shop?
Yes, creating an account allows you to track orders, save addresses, and manage your profile. Registration is quick and free.

Can I update my profile information?
Yes, you can update your personal information, email, and manage multiple delivery addresses from your profile page.

Browsing & Searching

How do I browse products?
You can browse by categories (Tables, Chairs, Armchairs, Sofas) from the homepage or use the search bar to find specific items. We also display bestsellers and new arrivals.

Can I filter products?
Yes, in our catalog you can filter by price range and view bestsellers. Use the search function to find products by name or specifications.

Shopping & Cart

How do I add items to my cart?
Click the "Add to Cart" button on any product. You'll need to be logged in. Your cart is saved with your account.

Can I modify items in my cart?
Yes, you can update quantities or remove items from your cart at any time before checkout.

Do you offer discounts?
Yes, we regularly feature discounted products. Bestseller items are also highlighted for special attention.

Ordering & Checkout

How do I place an order?
Add items to your cart, review your items, select or add a delivery address, and proceed to payment.

How do I manage delivery addresses?
You can save multiple addresses in your profile. Select the address you want during checkout or create a new one.

Can I view my order history?
Yes, log into your account to see all your past and current orders with their status and details.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for 2-3 business day delivery.

Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location.

Can I track my order?
Yes, check your order status in your profile. We'll provide tracking information once your order ships.

Payment & Security

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and digital wallets. All transactions are secured with SSL encryption.

Is my payment information safe?
Yes, we use industry-standard security measures to protect your payment information.

Products & Returns

Can I modify my order after placing it?
Orders can be modified within 1 hour of placement. Contact our support team for assistance.

What if my item arrives damaged?
Contact us immediately with photos of the damage. We will replace or refund your item at no cost.

What is your return policy?
Visit our Returns page for detailed information about our return process and time limits.

Newsletter & Offers

How do I subscribe to the newsletter?
You can subscribe using the newsletter form in the footer. We'll send you updates on new products and promotions.

How do I unsubscribe?
Click the unsubscribe link in any newsletter email or update your preferences in your account settings.
